Customer Profiling: Identify the top 10 unique customers based on the total number of orders placed.
Sales Distribution: Using NumPy, determine the 25th, 50th (median), and 75th percentiles of Sales to understand the distribution of transaction sizes.

Level 2: Intermediate Manipulation & Logic

Monthly Trends: Aggregate total Sales by month and year. Create a bar chart showing the growth trend over the years.
Ship Mode Preference: Calculate the percentage of total orders that fall into each Ship Mode category.
Discount Impact: Create a scatter plot visualizing the relationship between Discount levels and Profit. Use a trend line to illustrate the impact of higher discounts on profitability.

Rolling Average: Calculate the 3-month rolling average of Sales for the entire store to smooth out seasonal volatility.
Running Total: Create a new column showing the cumulative running total of Sales over time (sorted by Order Date).
Rankings: For each Category, identify the top 3 Product Name items based on Profit
